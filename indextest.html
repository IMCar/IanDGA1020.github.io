Biography


My name is Ian Mark Caruana , 39 years old with great interest in sound design especially natural organic soundscapes. A passion for Digital Arts sparked since I felt the need to colour my compositions with a visual narrative. Whilst producing music and soundscapes my mind starts to create pictures and sometimes I can also visualize a story which takes over my composition driving the sounds being produced. In 2021 I decide to join the Department of Digital Arts at the University of Malta which has been an exceptional learning experience.

Sound design is the art of giving aural texture to visuals.

Music and sound production was once considered as a post visual process. This is a normal proceedure in the production of traditional filming. The sound design process is now adays being initiated by many production companies at concept phase especially in the creation of animation and games. Sound and music is like a binding agent which moulds the visuals. Sound may alter the viewers understanding of the visual dynamics in a scene. If your hear the sound of glass shattering, what does this trigger in your thoughts? The breaking sound could have many direct relations to the breaking of an actual object made of glass but it can also trigger an emotional condition that something irriversable has happened such as the end of a relationship.

I personally think of sound and music to be a complete phenomena because one can visualize his own creative story in his immagination. Visuals on their own effect our reseoning of heppennings but visuals with sound, effect our senses deeper giving more weight, volume and dynamics to a scene. Imagine you are watching a clip of a gunshot or a bomb exploding or else a high speed car crash without sound. Adding sound to the 3 examples will add and understanding of the type of gun being used, the severity and dimensions of the explotion, the speed and severity of the ipact.

Currently I am working with various visual design tools and experiencing a well structured tuition by experts in the field. My main interest is in the production of moving graphics such as film, animation and 3d.

The projects presented underneath are a selection of completed assignments using various design tools ranging from illustation to 3d modelling and animation. The technics used to achieve the results were primarily researched and later applied. In some of the projects more than one software and technique was used in order to learn how the practices interect together.
