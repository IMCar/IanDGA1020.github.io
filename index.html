Biography

My name is Ian Mark Caruana , 38 years old with great interest in sound especially natural organic soundscapes. A passion for science communication started since I got employed at Esplora Interactive Science Centre, Malta about 5 years ago. Last year I have won the Star Awards final as most nominated employee amongst the museums in Malta.


Lately I learnt that I am a cat person and big cat lover after saving 2 very young kittens (Marsh and Mellow) which lost their mum and were dehydrated due to a construction site that closed the access to their hiding place.


I consider myself having a very playful personality which shows best whilst hosting school visits at work and interacting with big groups inside the planetarium. Since Covid-19 has driven most education Online I felt the need to get my IT knowledge up to date with regards E-learning platforms and methodology. A short video titled Space Radio gives an idea of what I love doing most apart from chilling in nature playing instruments or recording organic sounds. Both music and video are self produced. Planetarium software was used to compose the images. Enjoy!
